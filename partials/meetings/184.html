Peer-2-Peer Salary Review

Jakub Nabrdalik

Termin: 20.09.2016 godz. 18:15

Lokalizacja: MIMUW, sala 3180

O prezentacji:

As a Head of Software Dev, part of my job was to review salaries every now and then. Except I don't think I'm the right guy to do it. You may be fair in a group of 7 devs you work with, but this doesn't scale. When you have ~40 people, it's pure science fiction. So I've created a simple process of peer-to-peer review, that allows me to get salaries more or less fairbring people some feedback, while not trying to play a semi-omnipotent judge, and which doesn't take forever.

This is a case study / tutorial, of how to optimize salaries for fairness, while being constrained by corporate rules, law, and lack of time.

It ain't perfect, but after Jurgen Appelo gave a lecture at Devoxx PL last year, one of my devs came to me happy, saying we do it right. So I guess it's not that bad either.
